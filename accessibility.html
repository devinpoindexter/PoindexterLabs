Accessibility Statement

Poindexter Labs, LLC is committed to providing a website that is usable by as many people as possible, including people using assistive technologies and people with visual, auditory, motor, or cognitive disabilities.

Accessibility Approach

We aim to maintain a simple static site with semantic headings, keyboard-accessible navigation, visible focus indicators, alternative text for meaningful images, and reduced-motion support where animation appears.

We use these principles to support common accessibility expectations under the ADA, Section 508-style best practices, and WCAG 2.2 Level AA guidance.

Measures Currently In Place

  • A skip link to main content.
  • Keyboard-focus styling for links and buttons.
  • Semantic heading structure on the main pages.
  • Decorative graphics marked as hidden from assistive technology where appropriate.
  • Reduced-motion handling for animated elements.
  • Plain-language policy pages that can be reached from the footer.

Known Limitations

Accessibility is an ongoing effort, not a one-time state. Some combinations of browsers, assistive technologies, zoom settings, or device-specific behavior may still present issues we have not yet identified.

Feedback And Alternative Formats

If you encounter an accessibility barrier on this site, or need information in an alternative format, contact us and describe the page and issue you experienced. We will make reasonable efforts to respond and provide an accessible alternative.

Accessibility contact: contact Poindexter Labs.

Ongoing Review

We intend to review this site periodically for accessibility issues and update the site when material problems are found.
