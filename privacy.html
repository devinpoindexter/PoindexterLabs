Privacy Notice

This notice explains how Poindexter Labs, LLC handles personal data when you visit this website. It is written primarily to support common privacy expectations of our customers in the United States.

Who We Are

Poindexter Labs, LLC is the operator of this website and the controller for any personal data described in this notice.

Data We Collect Directly

We do not use account registration, checkout, analytics, advertising pixels, or contact forms on this website.

As a result, we do not intentionally collect personal data directly through on-page forms or trackers.

Data You May Provide Voluntarily

If you contact us by email, we will receive the information you include in your message, such as your name, email address, company name, and the contents of your request.

We use that information only to respond to your inquiry, evaluate a possible business relationship, or maintain related business records.

Automatically Processed Technical Data

Like most websites, requests to this site may generate technical logs through our hosting, security, and content delivery providers. Those logs can include IP address, browser information, timestamps, requested URLs, and similar network diagnostics.

We use that information for site delivery, security, abuse prevention, debugging, and service reliability.

Cookies And Similar Technologies

This website is designed not to use analytics cookies, advertising cookies, or similar tracking technologies.

If our hosting or security providers set strictly necessary cookies in the future for security or traffic management, we will update this notice to reflect that change.

How We Use Personal Data

  • To operate, secure, and maintain the website.
  • To respond to email inquiries and business requests.
  • To detect, prevent, or investigate misuse or technical issues.
  • To comply with legal obligations and protect legal rights.

Legal Bases For Processing

Where the UK GDPR, EU GDPR, or similar frameworks apply, our legal bases for processing personal data are:

  • Legitimate interests — operating, securing, and maintaining this website, and preventing misuse.
  • Legal obligation — complying with laws that require us to retain or disclose data.
  • Consent — where you have asked us to contact you or provided information voluntarily.

California Privacy Rights

We do not sell or share personal information for cross-context behavioral advertising through this website. We do not offer financial incentives tied to personal information.

We do not collect or use sensitive personal information as defined under the California Privacy Rights Act (CPRA), such as Social Security numbers, precise geolocation, financial account credentials, or health information.

We do not disclose personal information to third parties for their own direct marketing purposes (California Civil Code § 1798.83).

California residents may request access to, correction of, or deletion of personal information we hold about them, subject to applicable exceptions. To exercise these rights, use the contact link on this site.

Other Privacy Rights

Depending on applicable law, you may have rights to access, correct, delete, restrict, object to, or request a copy of personal data we hold about you. You may also have the right to complain to your local supervisory authority.

Data Sharing

We may share personal data only with service providers that help us host, secure, or operate this website, or when disclosure is required by law or needed to protect rights and safety.

We do not disclose personal data to data brokers or ad networks through this site.

Retention

We keep business correspondence only as long as reasonably necessary for the purpose collected, including follow-up communications, recordkeeping, legal compliance, or dispute resolution.

Technical logs are retained according to provider defaults or operational need.

International Transfers

Our service providers may process data in countries other than your own. When required, we rely on appropriate contractual, legal, or technical safeguards for those transfers.

Children's Privacy

This website is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal data from children through this site.

Changes To This Notice

We may update this notice from time to time to reflect legal, technical, or business changes. When we do, we will revise the "Last updated" date on this page.

Contact And Rights Requests

For privacy questions or rights requests, use the contact email link on this site: contact Poindexter Labs.
